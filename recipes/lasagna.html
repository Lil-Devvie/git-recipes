Lasagna

A delicious and savory lasagna! A recipe for the whole family!

You will only need a handful of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Peas
  • Applesauce
  • Something purple (preferably kosher)

Instructions

  1. Place oven in lasagna at room temperature
  2. Increase power to shields
  3. Purplize under supervision
  4. Reduce, plate, and exaggerate when eating
